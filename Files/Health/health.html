HOSPITAL MANAGEMENT (B.B.A)

About The Course

The hospital industry in India is accounting for 80% of the total healthcare market and is witnessing a huge investor demand from both global as well as domestic investors. The hospital industry is expected to reach $ 132 bn by 2023 from $ 61.8 bn in 2017; growing at a CAGR of 16-17%. The course BBA-Hospital management is a three year full time programme wherein the students get to know on how a Hospital operates and the role of Administration in hospital and allied health services. BBA in Hospital Management course covers general management subjects. Along with them, the course also covers subjects related to the field of healthcare and hospital management.

Course Level Graduation
Duration 3 years
Approving Authority Affiliated to M.A.K.A.U.T ( Formerly W.B.U.T)
Eligibility Graduation in any stream
Admission Process Merit based direct admission and/or a valid CET rank

Career Prospects

The career prospects of an BBA-Hospital Management program at SKFGI would have to undergo a rigorous academic engagement with parallel exposure to hospitals for real time situational learning. The students would be able to comfortably work on health care delivery systems along with the associated legal and regulatory environmental factors. The course makes the students ready to handle managerial positions with the capacity of assessing current needs and recommending appropriate solutions. This course at SKFGI also prepares the students to demonstrate leadership skills and face challenges of the dynamic nature of health care administration.

Most common sectors and jobs after BBA-Hospital Management are as follows:
  • Medical Superintendant
  • Adminsitrative Officer
  • Operation Executive
  • Patient Care Assistants
  • Clinical Adminstrrators


Bachelor Of Optometry

About The Course

According to the definition of World Council of Optometry (WCO), an organization that represents optometrists worldwide, “optometrists are the primary healthcare practitioners of the eye and visual system who provide comprehensive eye and vision care, which includes refraction and dispensing, detection/diagnosis and management of disease in the eye, and the rehabilitation of conditions of the visual system”. While, an estimated 456 million people in India require vision correction (spectacles, contact lenses or refractive surgery) to be able to see and function, the demand for optometrists is very high and is expected to rise further in the coming years. With enormous job opportunities in India and abroad, Optometry has come out as a lucrative carrier option through which financial growth, social respect, and professional satisfaction can be achieved.

Course Level Graduation
Duration 3 years
Approving Authority Affiliated to M.A.K.A.U.T ( Formerly W.B.U.T)
Eligibility 10+2 in Physics,Chemistry & ( Biology or Math ) as a subject from a recognized board.
Admission Process Merit based direct admission and/or a valid CET rank

Bachelor in Optometry and Vision Science at SKFGI:

The students in the Bachelor of Optometry program at SKFGI would be exposed to a very specialized environment of learning which is based on well equipped class rooms, laboratories on optics and clinical diagnosis. In total four years of optometry trainings, the initial three years would comprise of class room teaching, lab oriented training, hand-on with optometry instruments, observation of real case studies, industrial visits, conferences and works shops. To increase the quality of diagnosis of the students, an in-house optometry clinic has been set where variety of optometry specialties including comprehensive eye care, contact lens, low vision rehabilitation, binocular vision can be practiced.
In the final year of training, the student would be placed at tertiary eye care hospitals for clinical internship. Furthermore, students would be given opportunity to understand and participate in vision researches under the guidance of experienced mentors, competent in optometry and ophthalmology researches. Thus, an alumnus of SKFGI- Optometry, would not only be academically and clinically competent, but would also possess strong understanding of vision researches.

Placements :

They can work in eye hospitals, eye care organizations, optical chains, vision care industries, contact lens companies. Optometrists can also seek employment with any multinational organization dealing with eye care



Bachelor Of Medical Laboratory Technology

About The Course

This course is specifically meant for the professionals who want to make a career in diagnostic science and opting to know more about the national and international standards of the laboratory in detail. The course aims to provide the candidate with adequate knowledge and skills to carry out routine and some sophisticated laboratory diagnostic procedures. Students would acquire theoretical knowledge related to the principles of the laboratory exercises and will also learn to to deal with new advances in instrumentation, methodology or standardization, Management of Laboratories, Standard Protocols and use of technology that is related to Laboratory Investigations.
Medical laboratory technologists do routine laboratory testing under stipulated conditions. Technologists may prepare specimens and operate machines that automatically analyze samples. The medical technologists are an integral part of the medical profession and get involved in practical and technical work to aid correct diagnosis and in the effective functioning of Biochemical Laboratories.

Course Level Graduation
Duration 4 years
Approving Authority Affiliated to M.A.K.A.U.T ( Formerly W.B.U.T)
Eligibility 10+2 in Science stream ( Mathematics or Biology) with English as a subject from a recognized board.
Admission Process Merit based direct admission and/or a valid CET rank

Career Prospects

The career prospects in this field depend on the academic and technical skills of the technologist. These days with the growth in the private sector there are so many private hospitals, nursing homes, blood banks, pathology laboratories etc. the demand for laboratory technologists is on the up-swing.

They can work as:
  • Lab Technician
  • R&D Lab Assistant
  • Junior Technical Executive
  • Lab Medicine Technician
