BSC - Hospitality & Hotel Administration

About The Course

Hospitality being a global industry, gives a student a thriving profession which has enormous scope of career building, growth and stability. Hotel industry is not only a great source of employment, but also a great contributor in a development and growth of a country’s economy. This 3 year course of hotel management enables a student in exploring a new world of hospitality industry which includes hotels, resorts, restaurants, bars, cruise liners, aviation, event management, retails and many more sectors. Acquired knowledge and skill will provide students to choose their career option of being hotel-restaurant managers, chefs, bar tenders, front office and accommodation managers among many.
Backed by perfect blend of industry and academic expertise, the forte of the hospitality department is its industry – academia liaison. The curriculum includes practical training at the state-of-the-art laboratories designed at per with international standards. With a vision of making it a center of excellence, the Institute is committed to provide national and international training and placement assistance with no extra cost to students.

Course Level Graduation
Duration 3 years
Approving Authority Affiliated to M.A.K.A.U.T (Formerly W.B.U.T)
Eligibility 10+2 in any stream with English as a subject from a recognized board.
Admission Process Merit based direct admission and/or a valid CET rank

Career Prospects

The curriculum aims at holistic development of students to prepare them to take up the leadership role in the industry and withstand the pressure of the business world.

The passed-out students can make their career in :-
  • Hotels / Motels / Resorts
  • Restaurants / Bars
  • Cruise liners
  • Aviation industry
  • Railway Catering
  • Retail Industry
  • Event Management
  • Amusement Parks
  • Banking & Insurance Sector
  • BPO
  • Travel Agency
