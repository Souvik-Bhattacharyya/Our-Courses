Master of Business Administration (M.B.A)

About The Course

MBA course teaches students about various business and management skills. They learn how the corporate industry operates and how the business world works. The course aim is to make socially responsible managers capable of managing teams and providing valuable insights. They should work for the benefit of the organization and the customer service as a whole.
The regular MBA course includes mandatory internships during the breaks in both the years. It is done to enhance the practical knowledge and skills of the students.

Course Level Post Graduation
Duration 2 years
Approving Authority Approved by A.I.C.T.E & Affiliated to M.A.K.A.U.T ( Formerly W.B.U.T)
Eligibility Graduation in any stream
Admission Process Candidate should have a valid rank of MAT / WBJEMAT

Career Prospects

The career prospects of an MBA are unlimited in terms of opportunities and scope. A person has an option to become a manager or head in departments like operations, marketing, sales, supply chain and much more. There are job opportunities for almost every MBA Specialization.

Most common sectors and jobs after MBA are as follows:
  • Product and Brand Managers
  • Investment Banker
  • Media & Entertainment
  • Real Estate
  • Management Consultant


Bachelor of Business Administration (B.B.A)

About The Course

BBA course equips you with the right knowledge and skills in business and management and impart knowledge of the principles which are perfect for a career in the corporate world. A degree in Business Administration solidifies your base for your future Business and management career goals. Successful graduates of this course can opt for a range of jobs from sectors like finance, marketing, sales, management, government and education.
The course is designed in a way to train students effectively in management education and communication skills which further inculcates entrepreneurship skills. It also gives you a platform for pursuing courses like MBA. To do well in this field, students must possess leadership qualities, decision making skills, good oral and written communication skills.

Course Level Graduation
Duration 3 years
Approving Authority Affiliated to M.A.K.A.U.T ( Formerly W.B.U.T)
Eligibility 10+2 in any stream with English as a subject from a recognized board.
Admission Process Merit based direct admission and/or a valid CET rank

Career Prospects

After successful completion of Bachelor of Business Administation course, graduates can either opt for higher education or seek employment in different sectors. Generally, students pursue an MBA (Masters of Business Administration) course and specialize in a particular department or field of study. A combination of BBA and MBA is considered helpful in gaining constant promotions and high-salary job in the business world.

BBA course opens up a spectrum of job opportunities in the fields of Advertising, Academic Institutes, Banking, Consultancies, Foreign Exchange, Hotel Management, Manufacturing Industry, IT and ITES, Urban infrastructure & real estate management and various Government services.



Bachelor of Computer Applications (B.C.A)

About The Course

The growing importance of computers is now an established fact. Bachelor of Computer Applications degree ensures that you imbibed the right ingredients to make a career in the field of computers and technology. You don’t need a specialized engineering degree for this.
With the Bachelor of Computer Applications program, you can ensure deep learning of analytical skills, including analysis involved in computer systems and technology. What’s more, the program serves as a foundation for pursuing advanced studies in the field like the Master in Computer Applications (MCA) Program.

Course Level Graduation
Duration 3 years
Approving Authority Affiliated to M.A.K.A.U.T ( Formerly W.B.U.T)
Eligibility 10+2 passed in any stream with English and any one of Computers / IT / Mathematics / Business Maths as a subject from a recognized board.
Admission Process Merit based direct admission and/or a valid CET rank

Career Prospects

There are variety of career options that are available for a BCA graduate in IT industry. BCA degree holder can work in several sectors, some of which include Education, Multimedia, Financial Institutes, Government Departments, Security and Surveillance etc.

Candidates who don’t wish to go for a job after graduation, can also opt for higher studies like MCA degree i.e. Master of Computer Application.



Cyber Security

About The Course

Technology and computer systems are becoming ever more integrated with our day-to-day lives and with today's advancing technologies comes the increasing threat of cybercrime. The specialist techniques taught as part of this course will help prepare you for a career in a rapidly growing industry which is currently faced with a shortage of skills.

This course provides the opportunity to be at the forefront of defending cyberspace from sophisticated malicious attacks and future threats. This course help you learn the protection of computer equipment, information and services from unauthorised access, change or destruction. It will inform you of the latest technologies, help you understand legal, ethical and professional responsibilities and provide a broad awareness of the industry.

Course Level Graduation
Duration 3 years
Approving Authority Affiliated to M.A.K.A.U.T ( Formerly W.B.U.T)
Eligibility 10+2 with Mathematics/ Statistics/Business Mathematics/ Information Practice/ IT/ Computer Science/Computer Application and English as a subject from a recognized board.
Admission Process Merit based direct admission and/or a valid CET rank

Career Prospects

The course offers employment prospects in areas of network security, digital forensics, ethical hacking, cryptography, computer system security, bio-metrics, mobile phone security, etc.. It aims to provide candidates a foundation for preparing them in becoming professionals as well as empowered citizens who aid in protecting data and making the world cyber-attack free.
Cyber Security Graduates have excellent opportunities to pursue careers in industry, government, or law enforcement in roles such as a security analyst, penetration tester, forensic investigator, or cyber security engineer.



Bachelor of Multimedia, Animation & Graphics (B.C.A)

About The Course.

In simple terms, multimedia is content that uses a combination of different content forms – text, audio, images, animations, video and interactive content. Multimedia can be recorded, played, displayed, edited and interacted with. Multimedia content can be accessed through relevant information processing devices such as – TV, computer systems, game consoles and other electronic devices.
B.Sc. Multimedia, Animation & Graphics is a degree course that is suitable to develop skills in design, illustration, image manipulation, graphics designing, cinematography, photography, cartoons, 2D & 3D animation, video editing, visual effects and game designing and to develop creativity and conceptualizing ability.
Students will develop expertise in life drawing and related studies and apply audio and visual production techniques to an animation project. Students will develop a working vocabulary of art, design and visual communication technology, and develop a wider understanding of making computer graphics. The program comprises of the basics of animations and gaming projects.

Course Level Post Graduation
Duration 3 years
Approving Authority Affiliated to M.A.K.A.U.T (Formerly W.B.U.T)
Eligibility 10+2 in any stream with English as a subject from a recognized board.
Admission Process Merit based direct admission and/or a valid CET rank

Career Prospects

In order to build a rewarding career in animation and multimedia sector, one must have adequate work experience. One may start small and build an impressive work portfolio gradually. High profile recruiters are keen on recruiting professionals who have decent work experience, advanced skill set and an attractive portfolio under their belt.

  • Flim Production Frims
